About Society of Ubiquitous

Do you know the meaning of the word "Ubiquitous"? Ubiquitous is originally Latin word of religion. And it means "everyone can get benefit easily".

Nowadays, the society of "Ubiquitous" is pointed out. This shows the society via computer that everyone connects.

But the revolution of technology brought us to be able to use on not only computer but also portable device. We can use Internet easier than the time using computer. People can make more comfortable circumstances via networks. Also many companies develop many kinds of services to make it.
